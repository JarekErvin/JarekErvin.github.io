About


I am a writer, editor, and teacher.


I went to school at Temple University and University of Virginia. I’ve been an editor for iaspm-us.net and currently work as Managing Editor for Alternative Press magazine. Additionally, I worked as a teacher for a number of years, offering courses at Temple, UVa, Westminster Choir College, and Rowan University, and was also a research intern at Library of Congress.


My writing has appeared in a number of places, including Alternative Press, Popular Music & Society, Popular Music, In the Muse, and iaspm-us.net.


See Writing and Blog for samples of my work.
